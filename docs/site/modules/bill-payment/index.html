📋 Module Overview

Purpose: Allows cardholders to pay their account balance. Displays current balance, processes the payment, records it as a transaction, and updates the account balance.

Business Context: Bill payment closes the credit card lifecycle – after transactions accumulate a balance, cardholders pay down what they owe. Payments are recorded as transactions for audit and statement purposes.

Transaction: CB00  |  Program: COBIL00C  |  BMS Map: COBIL00

COBOL CICS VSAM ACCTFILE VSAM TRANSACT BMS COBIL00

🎯 User Story Templates

US-BILL-001: As a cardholder, I want to view my current account balance on the bill payment screen so that I know how much I owe before paying.
US-BILL-002: As a cardholder, I want to submit a payment so that my account balance is reduced.
US-BILL-003: As a cardholder, I want to receive a confirmation message after a successful payment so that I know my payment was processed.
US-BILL-004: As a cardholder, I want the system to reject a payment amount of zero or negative so that invalid payments are not processed.

✅ Acceptance Criteria Patterns

Payment Display:
GIVEN an authenticated cardholder with a valid account ID
WHEN CB00 is executed
THEN the current account balance is displayed accurately from ACCTFILE
Payment Success:
GIVEN the cardholder enters a valid positive payment amount
WHEN the payment is submitted
THEN ACCTFILE balance is reduced by the payment amount, a payment transaction is written to TRANSACT, and a success message is displayed
Payment Validation:
GIVEN the cardholder enters zero, negative, or non-numeric amount
WHEN the payment is submitted
THEN an error message is displayed and no updates are made

🔧 Technical Foundation

Key Components

ComponentTypeResponsibility
COBIL00CCICS ProgramBill payment controller – reads balance, validates payment, updates ACCTFILE and TRANSACT
COBIL00BMS MapScreen layout for bill payment (balance display, payment amount entry)
ACCTFILEVSAM KSDSAccount master – read for current balance; rewritten after payment
TRANSACTVSAM KSDSTransaction master – payment written as transaction record

Payment Processing Flow

  1. Cardholder starts CB00 transaction
  2. COBIL00C reads ACCTFILE to get ACCT-CURR-BAL
  3. Displays balance on BMS map COBIL00
  4. Cardholder enters payment amount and presses Enter
  5. Program validates amount (> 0, numeric, ≤ balance)
  6. Updates ACCT-CURR-BAL in ACCTFILE (CICS REWRITE)
  7. Writes payment as transaction record to TRANSACT
  8. Displays success confirmation

Business Rules

  • Payment amount must be > 0
  • Payment is recorded in TRANSACT for reporting and statement purposes
  • Account balance is reduced by the payment amount
  • CUST-EFT-ACCOUNT-ID in customer record identifies the external payment account (not processed online in base app)

Dependencies

  • authentication: CICS session and account ID from commarea
  • accounts: ACCTFILE record updated during payment
  • transactions: Payment written to TRANSACT; appears in transaction list

⚡ Development Acceleration Factors

  • CVACT01Y copybook: Account record structure already defined – reuse ACCT-CURR-BAL field
  • CVTRA05Y copybook: Transaction record structure for writing payment transaction
  • Dual VSAM update pattern: COBIL00C demonstrates updating two VSAM files atomically in CICS – reuse this pattern for other write operations
